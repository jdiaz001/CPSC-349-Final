About Me

My name is Jose Diaz, and I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at California State University, Fullerton, with an expected graduation in Spring 2025. My academic journey has been shaped by a deep interest in technology, software development, and the ever-evolving field of computer science. I have completed a wide range of technical courses that have equipped me with a strong foundation in programming, systems, and web development. These include Object-Oriented Programming, Operating System Concepts, Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Web Development (both front-end and back-end), and Cybersecurity.

My practical experience has centered around real-world projects that reflect both my technical capabilities and collaborative skills. I developed TextBookTrade, a full-stack web application using Flask and MySQL that allows users to create and manage textbook listings with images and messaging functionality. I've also contributed to building Tuffle, a Wordle-inspired game using object-oriented principles, and designed a UN-based database project that involved ER modeling, querying, and analysis. Through these projects, I’ve become proficient in using technologies such as Python, Java, C++, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Flask, and MySQL.

In addition to my academic work, I’ve held various roles in fast-paced environments including Amazon, Home Chef, CTDI, and Target, where I gained valuable soft skills such as time management, teamwork, adaptability, and effective communication. These experiences taught me how to stay organized under pressure and collaborate efficiently with others, skills that complement my technical knowledge.
